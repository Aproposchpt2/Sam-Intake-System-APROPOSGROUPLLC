Lead Manager Connection

Intake becomes action inside FlowDesk Lead Manager.

FlowDesk Intake Engine captures structured customer information. FlowDesk Lead Manager organizes that information into records that can be reviewed, updated, and acted on from one dashboard.

Current managed actions

Email Lead supports a managed follow-up email action. Callback Task creates a managed follow-up task, updates the lead status, and records the next action for the team.

Callback Task does not place an automatic outbound call. The manual Call link remains available for human follow-up.

Review queue

Lead Manager helps the business scan submitted requests by status, source, urgency, and follow-up needs.

Record tracking

Internal notes, next action, status, and follow-up state give each intake record a clearer operating history.
